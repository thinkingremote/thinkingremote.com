Thinking Remote is a place to learn and share information about working remotely. Internet connectivity allows a growing number of people to work remotely without the need to ever set foot in an office. But telecommuting is not for everyone, it has its fair share of pros and cons.

Our mission as two lifelong remoters is to explore all the different aspects of this relatively new lifestyle. We hope to create a knowledge-rich platform which will allow remoters and those who consider becoming remote workers to exchange ideas, find solutions, answer questions, and contribute to the ever growing community of remote workers.
